I'm a UI/UX Designer

I'm a Mercy college Alumi with Bachelors Degree in computer design. After graduating from college I found a job at a Home Automation Company for their graphic design department. At my job I help design icons for our remote control user interfaces. After 3 years at my current position I started to feel unfulfilled, I wasn't learning anything new and it became routine. The skills I have are outdated since my current job is not using modern software which has hindered my learning process. So this past summer, I took the challenge and took the UI/UX design course at NYCDA. This was the best decision I ever made, I learned so much and feel this is what I needed to refresh my skills. Right now I am taking the Web Development course at NYCDA its a bit challenging for me, but I hope to learn all I can to create websites.
